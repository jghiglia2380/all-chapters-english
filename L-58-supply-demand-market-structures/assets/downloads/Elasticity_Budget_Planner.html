Budget Protection Strategies

  • Build emergency fund for inelastic price spikes
  • Keep elastic spending flexible - first to cut in crisis
  • Look for substitutes for inelastic goods (public transit, generics)
  • Lock in prices when possible (fuel cards, subscription annual plans)
