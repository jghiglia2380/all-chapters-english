# Asset Specifications for L-58: Supply, Demand, and Market Structures

## Overview
This chapter includes 8 HTML assets for the skill builder and Day 2 activities.

---

## Day 1 Skill Builder

### 1. Integrated Market Analysis Tool
- **Purpose:** Comprehensive tool for manipulating supply-demand curves and comparing market structures
- **Format:** Interactive web application
- **HTML File:** Integrated_Market_Analysis_Tool.html

---

## Day 2 Activity Assets

### 2. Market Dynamics Simulator (Equilibrium Challenge)
- **Purpose:** Shift supply and demand curves to observe equilibrium changes in various scenarios
- **Format:** Interactive curve manipulation simulator
- **HTML File:** Market_Dynamics_Simulator.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 1 - Market Equilibrium Challenge

### 3. Market Structure Comparison Tool
- **Purpose:** Compare prices, surplus, and welfare across perfect competition, oligopoly, and monopoly structures
- **Format:** Interactive comparison simulator
- **HTML File:** Market_Structure_Comparison.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 2 - Market Structure Comparison Station

### 4. Elasticity Budget Planner
- **Purpose:** Understand how demand elasticity affects price volatility and plan budgets for elastic vs. inelastic goods
- **Format:** Interactive budget planning tool
- **HTML File:** Elasticity_Budget_Planner.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 3 - Elasticity and Budget Planning

### 5. Real Market Intelligence Gatherer
- **Purpose:** Apply supply-demand analysis to actual planned purchases with market structure and timing analysis
- **Format:** Interactive research/planning tool
- **HTML File:** Market_Intelligence_Gatherer.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 4 - Real Market Intelligence Gathering

### 6. Consumer Surplus Maximization Game
- **Purpose:** Practice strategies to capture more consumer surplus across various purchasing scenarios
- **Format:** Interactive decision game
- **HTML File:** Consumer_Surplus_Game.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 5 - Consumer Surplus Maximization Game

### 7. Reference Sheet
- **Purpose:** Quick reference for supply-demand concepts, market structures, surplus formulas, elasticity
- **Format:** 1-page printable
- **HTML File:** Reference_Sheet.html

### 8. Supporting Worksheet
- **Purpose:** Curve shifting scenarios, market structure identification, surplus calculations
- **Format:** 2-page printable
- **HTML File:** Supporting_Worksheet.html
