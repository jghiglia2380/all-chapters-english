Understanding Deductions vs. Credits
Tax Deductions
Reduces taxable income
  • Savings = Deduction x Tax Rate
  • $1,000 deduction in 22% bracket = $220 saved
  • Standard vs. Itemized choice
  • More valuable at higher tax brackets
Tax Credits
Reduces tax owed directly
  • Dollar-for-dollar reduction
  • $1,000 credit = $1,000 saved
  • Refundable vs. Non-refundable
  • Equal value at all tax brackets
