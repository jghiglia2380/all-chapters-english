# Asset Specifications for L-68: Tax-Efficient Investing Strategies

## Overview
This chapter includes 8 HTML assets for the skill builder and Day 2 activities.

---

## Day 1 Skill Builder

### 1. Tax Efficiency Optimizer
- **Purpose:** Primary skill builder for optimizing investment tax efficiency
- **Format:** Interactive web application
- **HTML File:** Tax_Efficient_Investment_Planner.html

---

## Day 2 Activity Assets

### 2. Account Type Comparison Calculator
- **Purpose:** Calculate and compare after-tax wealth in Roth, traditional, and taxable accounts over 40 years
- **Format:** Interactive calculator
- **HTML File:** Account_Type_Comparison_Calculator.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 1 - Account Type Tax Impact Calculator

### 3. Asset Location Optimizer
- **Purpose:** Maximize after-tax returns through strategic asset placement with 30-year projections
- **Format:** Interactive optimization tool
- **HTML File:** Asset_Location_Optimizer.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 2 - Asset Location Optimization Challenge

### 4. Tax-Loss Harvesting Simulator
- **Purpose:** Practice identifying harvesting opportunities, calculating benefits, and selecting replacements while avoiding wash sales
- **Format:** Interactive simulation
- **HTML File:** Tax_Loss_Harvesting_Simulator.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 3 - Tax-Loss Harvesting Simulation

### 5. Capital Gains Timing Tool
- **Purpose:** Understand holding period optimization and strategic gain recognition in different scenarios
- **Format:** Interactive calculator/scenario tool
- **HTML File:** Capital_Gains_Timing_Tool.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 4 - Capital Gains Timing Strategy Game

### 6. Personal Tax Strategy Planner
- **Purpose:** Create personalized tax-efficient strategy with account selection, asset location, and commitments
- **Format:** Interactive planning tool
- **HTML File:** Personal_Tax_Strategy_Planner.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 5 - Build Your Personal Tax-Efficient Strategy

---

## Supporting Materials

### 8. Supporting Worksheet
