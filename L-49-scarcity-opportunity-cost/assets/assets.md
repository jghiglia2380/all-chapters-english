# Asset Specifications for L-49: Scarcity, Opportunity Cost, and Incentives

## Overview
This chapter includes 6 HTML assets for the skill builder and Day 2 activities.

---

## Day 1 Skill Builder

### 1. Opportunity Cost Calculator
- **Purpose:** Interactive tool for comparing major financial decisions showing explicit costs, opportunity costs, and total economic costs
- **Format:** Interactive web application
- **HTML File:** Opportunity_Cost_Calculator.html

---

## Day 2 Activity Assets

### 2. College vs. Alternative Paths Worksheet
- **Purpose:** Calculate and compare full economic costs (explicit + implicit) of college, trade school, and immediate workforce paths with 10-year projections
- **Format:** Interactive comparison calculator with tables
- **HTML File:** College_Alternative_Paths_Worksheet.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 1 - College vs. Alternative Paths Opportunity Cost Analysis (20 minutes)

### 3. Monthly Budget Opportunity Cost Analyzer
- **Purpose:** Allocate discretionary income across savings, investments, and spending categories while analyzing opportunity costs for each allocation decision
- **Format:** Interactive budget worksheet with opportunity cost calculations
- **HTML File:** Budget_Opportunity_Cost_Analyzer.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 2 - Monthly Budget Opportunity Cost Analysis (15 minutes)

### 4. Incentive Design Worksheet
- **Purpose:** Create personalized incentive systems with positive, negative, structural, and social incentives to achieve financial goals
- **Format:** Interactive goal-setting and incentive planning tool
- **HTML File:** Incentive_Design_Worksheet.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 3 - Incentive Design for Personal Goals (10 minutes)

### 5. State Policy Incentives Analyzer
- **Purpose:** Analyze how state government policies create incentives in education, housing, employment, environment, and retirement that affect personal finance
- **Format:** Interactive policy analysis worksheet
- **HTML File:** State_Policy_Incentives_Analyzer.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 4 - State Policy Incentives Analysis (10 minutes)

### 6. Cost Types Comparison Reference
- **Purpose:** Quick reference for opportunity cost formula, explicit vs. implicit costs, and incentive types
- **Format:** Printable reference document
- **HTML File:** Cost_Types_Comparison.html
- **Supports Activity:** All activities - provides reference information
