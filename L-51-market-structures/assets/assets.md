# Asset Specifications for L-51: Market Structures and Consumer Choice

## Overview
This chapter includes 7 HTML assets for the skill builder and Day 2 activities.

---

## Day 1 Skill Builder

### 1. Market Structure Identifier Tool
- **Purpose:** Systematic decision tree for classifying any market and developing consumer strategies based on structure type
- **Format:** Interactive web application
- **HTML File:** Market_Structure_Identifier_Tool.html

---

## Day 2 Activity Assets

### 2. Personal Purchase Market Classifier
- **Purpose:** Analyze 10 regular purchases by number of options, product differentiation, negotiation ability, and consumer power
- **Format:** Interactive classification worksheet
- **HTML File:** Purchase_Market_Classifier.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 1 - Market Structure Identification (15 minutes)

### 3. Consumer Strategy Worksheets
- **Purpose:** Develop specific strategies for maximizing value in each market structure: perfect competition, monopolistic competition, oligopoly, and monopoly
- **Format:** Interactive multi-section strategy builder with action plans
- **HTML File:** Consumer_Strategy_Worksheets.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 2 - Consumer Strategy Development (20 minutes)

### 4. State Industry Analyzer
- **Purpose:** Classify major state industries by market structure and analyze government regulation impact on consumers
- **Format:** Interactive industry classification and analysis tool
- **HTML File:** State_Industry_Analyzer.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 3 - State Industry Analysis (10 minutes)

### 5. Market Structure Comparison Table
- **Purpose:** Side-by-side comparison of all four market structures with characteristics, examples, and consumer strategies
- **Format:** Interactive comparison reference
- **HTML File:** Market_Structure_Comparison_Table.html
- **Supports Activity:** All activities - provides comparison reference

### 6. Market Structures Reference Sheet
- **Purpose:** Quick reference for four market structures, barriers to entry, and consumer strategies
- **Format:** Printable reference document
- **HTML File:** Reference_Sheet.html
- **Supports Activity:** All activities - provides reference information

### 7. Supporting Practice Worksheet
- **Purpose:** Practice exercises for structure classification, consumer surplus scenarios, and negotiation planning
- **Format:** Printable worksheet
- **HTML File:** Supporting_Worksheet.html
- **Supports Activity:** Additional practice and reinforcement
