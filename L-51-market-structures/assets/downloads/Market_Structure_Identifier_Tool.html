Market Structure Identifier Tool

Systematically classify any market and develop consumer strategies

How to Use This Tool

Follow the decision tree to identify the market structure for any product or service. The tool will provide specific consumer strategies based on the structure type.

Step 1: What Are You Analyzing?

Enter the product or service you want to classify

Step 2: How Many Sellers?

Count the number of realistic options available to you

One Seller
Only one provider exists - no alternatives available
Few Sellers (2-10)
A small number of major players dominate the market
Many Sellers (Dozens+)
Many companies compete for your business
Monopoly Verification

Confirm this is truly a monopoly

No Substitutes Available
This is the only option that meets my needs
Some Substitutes Exist
I could use something else as an alternative

Why does this monopoly exist?

Natural Monopoly (Infrastructure)
Electric, water, gas utilities - infrastructure costs prevent competition
Legal Monopoly (Patent/License)
Protected by patents, copyrights, or government licenses
Market Power (Dominance)
Achieved through business practices and market control
Oligopoly Verification

Check for barriers to entry

High Barriers to Entry
Hard for new companies to enter (high capital, regulations, patents, scale)
Low Barriers to Entry
New companies could enter relatively easily
Product Differentiation

Are products identical or different?

Identical Products
All sellers offer the exact same thing (commodities)
Differentiated Products
Each seller offers something slightly different (brands, features, quality)
