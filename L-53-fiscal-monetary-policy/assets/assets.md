# Asset Specifications for L-53: Fiscal and Monetary Policy

## Overview
This chapter includes 7 HTML assets for the skill builder and Day 2 activities.

---

## Day 1 Skill Builder

### 1. Policy Impact Simulator
- **Purpose:** Simulate fiscal and monetary policy decisions and see predicted outcomes
- **Format:** Interactive web application
- **HTML File:** Policy_Impact_Simulator.html

---

## Day 2 Activity Assets

### 2. Federal Reserve Decision Analysis Tool
- **Purpose:** Analyze Fed economic data and make interest rate decisions based on indicators
- **Format:** Interactive calculator/worksheet
- **HTML File:** Fed_Decision_Analysis.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 1 - Federal Reserve Decision Analysis

### 3. Federal Budget Analyzer
- **Purpose:** Evaluate government spending priorities and make budget trade-off decisions
- **Format:** Interactive calculator/worksheet
- **HTML File:** Federal_Budget_Analyzer.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 2 - Federal Budget Analysis

### 4. Interest Rate Personal Finance Calculator
- **Purpose:** Calculate how interest rate changes affect mortgages, auto loans, savings, and credit cards
- **Format:** Interactive calculator
- **HTML File:** Interest_Rate_Calculator.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 3 - Interest Rates and Personal Finances

### 5. Policy Scenario Simulator
- **Purpose:** Simulate combined fiscal and monetary policy decisions and see economic outcomes
- **Format:** Interactive simulation
- **HTML File:** Policy_Scenario_Simulator.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 4 - Policy Impact Simulator (Skill Builder)

### 6. Reference Sheet
- **Purpose:** Quick reference for fiscal and monetary policy concepts
- **Format:** 1-page printable
- **HTML File:** Reference_Sheet.html

### 7. Supporting Worksheet
- **Purpose:** Practice exercises for policy analysis
- **Format:** 2-page printable
- **HTML File:** Supporting_Worksheet.html
