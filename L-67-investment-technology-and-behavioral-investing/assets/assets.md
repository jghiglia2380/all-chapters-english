# Asset Specifications for L-67: Investment Technology and Behavioral Investing

## Overview
This chapter includes 8 HTML assets for the skill builder and Day 2 activities.

---

## Day 1 Skill Builder

### 1. Investment Platform Evaluator
- **Purpose:** Primary skill builder for comparing and evaluating investment platforms
- **Format:** Interactive web application
- **HTML File:** Behavioral_Bias_Checker.html

---

## Day 2 Activity Assets

### 2. Platform Deep Dive Comparison Tool
- **Purpose:** Compare three investment platforms (Betterment, Fidelity Go, Fidelity Self-Directed) with 30-year cost projections
- **Format:** Interactive comparison/calculator
- **HTML File:** Platform_Deep_Dive_Comparison.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 1 - Platform Deep Dive Comparison

### 3. Behavioral Bias Self-Assessment Quiz
- **Purpose:** Identify personal behavioral bias susceptibility through scenario-based questions with detailed results
- **Format:** Interactive assessment/quiz
- **HTML File:** Behavioral_Bias_Self_Assessment.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 2 - Behavioral Bias Self-Assessment

### 4. Investment Decision Simulator
- **Purpose:** Navigate three scenarios (market crash, hot stock, confirmation trap) and experience bias effects
- **Format:** Interactive simulation with decision points
- **HTML File:** Investment_Decision_Simulator.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 3 - Investment Decision Simulator

### 5. Bias Counter Rules Builder
- **Purpose:** Create personal rules for buying, selling, and portfolio management to counter behavioral biases
- **Format:** Interactive planning tool
- **HTML File:** Bias_Counter_Rules_Builder.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 4 - Build Your Bias-Counter System

### 6. Long-Term Cost Visualizer
- **Purpose:** Visualize 40-year compounding impact of different fee levels on portfolio growth
- **Format:** Interactive calculator/visualizer
- **HTML File:** Long_Term_Cost_Visualizer.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 5 - Long-Term Cost Visualization
