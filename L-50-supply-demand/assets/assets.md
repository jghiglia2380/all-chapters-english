# Asset Specifications for L-50: Supply and Demand in Personal Finance

## Overview
This chapter includes 6 HTML assets for the skill builder and Day 2 activities.

---

## Day 1 Skill Builder

### 1. Market Analyzer Tool
- **Purpose:** Systematic framework for analyzing any market's supply and demand factors with predictions and personal action plans
- **Format:** Interactive web application
- **HTML File:** Market_Analyzer_Tool.html

---

## Day 2 Activity Assets

### 2. Housing Market Analysis Worksheet
- **Purpose:** Analyze state housing market supply and demand factors, predict market equilibrium, and develop personal strategies for renting, buying, or investing
- **Format:** Interactive worksheet with factor analysis and predictions
- **HTML File:** Housing_Market_Analysis_Worksheet.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 1 - State Housing Market Analysis (20 minutes)

### 3. Labor Market Career Analyzer
- **Purpose:** Analyze career fields for demand/supply factors, market outlook, salary trajectories, and AI/remote work impact scenarios
- **Format:** Interactive career comparison tool
- **HTML File:** Labor_Market_Career_Analyzer.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 2 - State Labor Market Analysis (15 minutes)

### 4. Price Elasticity Worksheet
- **Purpose:** Classify goods as elastic or inelastic, develop purchase timing strategies, and analyze personal leverage for major purchases
- **Format:** Interactive classification tool with strategy generator
- **HTML File:** Price_Elasticity_Worksheet.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 3 - Price Elasticity and Purchase Timing (10 minutes)

### 5. Supply Demand Reference Sheet
- **Purpose:** Quick reference for laws of supply and demand, shifters, equilibrium, and elasticity basics
- **Format:** Printable reference document
- **HTML File:** Reference_Sheet.html
- **Supports Activity:** All activities - provides reference information

### 6. Supporting Practice Worksheet
- **Purpose:** Practice exercises for supply/demand curve analysis, shifter identification, and market scenarios
- **Format:** Printable worksheet
- **HTML File:** Supporting_Worksheet.html
- **Supports Activity:** Additional practice and reinforcement
