  • The wider the spread, the more costly the trade
  • High-volume stocks have tighter spreads (lower cost)

Module 3: Limit Order Practice

Practice using limit orders to control the price you pay or receive.

Place a Limit Order

ðŸ’¡ How Limit Orders Work:

  • Buy Limit: Only buys if price drops to your limit or below
  • Sell Limit: Only sells if price rises to your limit or above
  • Benefit: Control your price, potentially save money
  • Risk: Order may never execute if price doesn't reach your limit
