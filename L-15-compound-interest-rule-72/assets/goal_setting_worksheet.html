Part 2: Savings Calculation for Short-Term Goal

For your short-term goal, you'll calculate:

Lump Sum Calculation

Use this formula to calculate how much you would need to invest as a single deposit to reach your goal:

Lump Sum = Target Amount ÷ (1 + r)t

Where r is the annual interest rate (as a decimal) and t is the time in years.

Monthly Contribution Calculation

If you can't invest a lump sum, calculate how much you would need to save monthly:

Monthly Contribution = Target Amount ÷ [((1 + r/12)n - 1) ÷ (r/12)]

Where r is the annual interest rate (as a decimal) and n is the total number of months.
