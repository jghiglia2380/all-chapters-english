b) What percentage of funds goes directly to programs?

Part 3: Identifying Red Flags
Exercise 3: Spotting Fraudulent Charities

Review this scenario and identify the warning signs.

You receive a phone call from "Veterans Support Foundation" asking for an immediate donation. The caller says they need your credit card number right away because a matching donor will double your gift, but only if you donate in the next 10 minutes. When you ask for their website, they give you a URL that's similar to a well-known veterans charity but slightly different.

a) List ALL the red flags in this scenario:
b) What should you do instead of providing your credit card information?
c) Where would you report a suspected fraudulent charity?
Key Red Flags: High-pressure tactics, requests for immediate payment, similar names to legitimate charities, refusal to provide written information, no verifiable tax ID.
