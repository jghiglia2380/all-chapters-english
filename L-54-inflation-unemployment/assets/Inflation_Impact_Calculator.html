Protection Strategies

  • Invest in assets that grow faster than inflation (stocks, real estate)
  • Consider Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS)
  • Negotiate raises that beat inflation (3-5% annually)
  • Avoid keeping large amounts in low-interest savings
  • Lock in fixed-rate debts when inflation is high
