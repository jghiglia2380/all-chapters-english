# Asset Specifications for L-65: Investment Portfolio Diversification

## Overview
This chapter includes 7 HTML assets for the skill builder and Day 2 activities.

---

## Day 1 Skill Builder

### 1. Portfolio Diversification Builder
- **Purpose:** Primary skill builder for constructing diversified portfolios
- **Format:** Interactive web application
- **HTML File:** Diversification_Analyzer.html

---

## Day 2 Activity Assets

### 2. Correlation Matrix Analyzer
- **Purpose:** Examine historical correlation data between asset classes and test portfolios through historical periods
- **Format:** Interactive analysis tool
- **HTML File:** Correlation_Matrix_Analyzer.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 1 - Correlation Detective Challenge

### 3. Asset Allocation Optimizer
- **Purpose:** Design appropriate allocations for investors at different life stages (Maya 25, David 45, Patricia 60)
- **Format:** Interactive optimizer/calculator
- **HTML File:** Asset_Allocation_Optimizer.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 2 - Age-Based Allocation Strategy Builder

### 4. Market Crisis Survival Simulator
- **Purpose:** Build portfolios and see how they perform through simulated major market crisis with decision points
- **Format:** Interactive simulation
- **HTML File:** Market_Crisis_Simulator.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 3 - Market Crisis Survival Simulation

### 5. Rebalancing Practice Simulator
- **Purpose:** Manage portfolio over 10 simulated years with annual rebalancing decisions and outcome comparison
- **Format:** Interactive simulation
- **HTML File:** Rebalancing_Practice_Simulator.html
- **Supports Activity:** Activity 4 - Rebalancing Practice Lab

---

## Supporting Materials

### 6. Reference Sheet

### 7. Supporting Worksheet
