Part 3: Opportunity Assessment
Exercise 3: Evaluate Your Business Idea

Use this framework to assess your idea from Part 2:

a) Problem Validation: What specific problem does your idea solve? How painful is this problem?
b) Target Market: Who has this problem? Estimate the market size.
c) Competition: Who else solves this problem? What's your unique approach?
d) Feasibility: What skills, resources, and time would you need?
e) Profitability: What would you charge? What are your costs? Is there profit margin?
