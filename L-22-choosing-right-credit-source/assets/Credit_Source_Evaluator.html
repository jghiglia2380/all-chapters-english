Warning: Predatory Lending

Payday loans and some high-interest lenders can trap borrowers in debt cycles. A $500 payday loan can cost over $1,500 if rolled over multiple times. Always explore alternatives like credit union emergency loans, payment plans with creditors, or community assistance programs first.
