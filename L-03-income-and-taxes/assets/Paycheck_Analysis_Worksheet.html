Chapter 1.3: Income and Taxes

Understanding the Components of a Paycheck

This worksheet will help you analyze sample paychecks to understand the relationship between gross income and net income, identify different types of deductions, and calculate the impact of those deductions on take-home pay.

Key Terms Review

  • Gross Income: The total amount earned before any deductions are taken out
  • Net Income: The amount actually received after all deductions (take-home pay)
  • Mandatory Deductions: Required deductions such as federal income tax, state income tax, and FICA taxes (Social Security and Medicare)
  • Voluntary Deductions: Optional deductions such as retirement contributions, health insurance premiums, and other benefits
