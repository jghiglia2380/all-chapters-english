Chapter 3.1: Understanding Financial Service Providers

Instructions

Research Assignment: In this activity, you will research and compare financial service providers to better understand their offerings, fees, and features. This research will help you make informed recommendations in later activities.

Steps:

  1. Select a type of financial service provider to research (bank, credit union, etc.)
  2. Choose 2-3 specific providers of that type to compare
  3. Complete a research form for each provider
  4. Create a comparison summary highlighting key differences

Helpful Research Resources:

  • Provider websites (look for "About Us," "Products & Services," "Fees," and "Account Types" sections)
  • Consumer review sites (Nerdwallet, Bankrate, Consumer Reports)
  • Government resources (FDIC, NCUA, Consumer Financial Protection Bureau)
  • Financial comparison tools (checking account comparison sites, loan rate finders)
